The game automatically generates nine random numbers every one minute. The numbers are 1-9. A number can appear more than once in a single game or can miss to appear. You therefore need to predict from the markets above whether the numbers you chose will appear ar not depending on the market you chose.
For example, if you chose that numbers 2 4 7 will appear in the case of market number one for example, then all of them must appear if the numbers are generated to win otherwise even if one of the three numbers miss to a ppear you lose. on the other hand if you for example bet on numbers not to appear in this case market number 2, then non of the numbers you chose should appear to win otherwise you lose. Different Markets have different Odds depenging on how easy or hard the prediction is.
After you decide on the market to bet on, click it's box and proceed to click the numbers you want to bet on, input your stake, refresh your stake (DO NOT REFRESH THE PAGE) and sit back to wait the timer to finish counting and you will be shown you bet results. In case if a win, you can decide to withdraw all of your money or keep it or continue with the game. You can also withdraw your deposite if you decide not to play.
